Implements the scaled dot-product attention.

Scaled dot-product attention is Eq. (1) in the transformer paper [3] :

\[\text{Attention}(Q, K, V) = \text{softmax}\left(\frac{QK^\top}{\sqrt{d_k}}\right)V.\]
